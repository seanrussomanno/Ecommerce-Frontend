Semantic Elements

HTML5 introduced many semantic elements that provide meaning to the structure:

  • <header> - For the top of a page or section
  • <nav> - For navigation menus
  • <main> - For the main content area
  • <article> - For self-contained content
  • <section> - For thematic grouping of content
  • <aside> - For content tangentially related to the main content
  • <footer> - For the bottom of a page or section
